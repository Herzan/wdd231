Festival au Désert

Traditionally held in January, this iconic festival celebrates peace and unity through music and cultural exchange. It features performances by renowned artists, including the Tuareg band Tinariwen, and showcases the region's rich musical heritage!

Contact Cynthia Jones for more information about getting involved at 208-458-0001.
