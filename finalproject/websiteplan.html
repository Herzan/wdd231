Nicaragua Adventure - Site Plan

Site Name

Nicaragua Adventure - The name reflects the purpose of the website: to showcase the exciting outdoor activities and travel experiences available in Nicaragua.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inspire and guide travelers who want to explore Nicaragua’s natural beauty through hiking, biking, rafting, and extreme sports. The site will provide destination guides, travel tips, and recommended gear for adventures.

Scenarios

  • What are the top hiking trails in Nicaragua and what equipment should I bring?
  • Where can I find safe and affordable adventure tour operators in Nicaragua?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used across the site:

  • Primary Color: #00695C (Teal Green) – used for headers, navigation, and buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #FFA726 (Sunset Orange) – used for highlights, call-to-action areas, and section backgrounds.

Typography

Font: "Roboto", sans-serif.

Headings will use bold Roboto for clarity, while paragraphs will use regular Roboto for readability.
